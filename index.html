How It Works

1

Capture

Every session begins with a camera snapshot or video feed. Our system captures facial landmarks to analyze expressions.

2

Decode

We run deep neural networks on‑device to map your expression to emotions like joy, calm, surprise or stress.

3

Insights

Aggregated results show individual feelings and team trends, enabling leaders to nurture well‑being.

Privacy & Ethics

We respect your emotional data. All processing occurs locally—no raw images leave your device. Results are anonymized before being aggregated into group insights. Our models focus on expressions, not mind‑reading. We acknowledge cultural bias and continuously audit for fairness.
